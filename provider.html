Unum offers access to a large national network of independent vision providers and optical retail outlets. We encourage you to contact us or your selected vision provider prior to visiting their location to confirm they participate in the network listed on your ID card.

Walmart locations will not extend discounts on frames above the plan allowance, lens options, contact lenses or contact lens fittings. Some vision provider professionals may not offer discounts on prestige frames or amounts over the allowed benefit. For information on how to use your benefits at Costco Optical locations, please log in to AlwaysAssist.

Our network is constantly growing and changing, and we strive to maintain an accurate and updated listing of vision provider professionals. If you notice any information that needs revision, pleas notify us by calling (888) 400-9304 or emailing VisionDirectory@Unum.com. If your vision provider is not listed, refer them here.
